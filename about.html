About Enzur Ethio-Travel Agency

Discover the story behind Ethiopia's most trusted travel agency

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Addis Ababa, Enzur-Ethio Travel Agency began with a simple mission: to share the authentic beauty of Ethiopia with the world. Our name, "Enzur-Ethio," combines the Amharic word for "journey" with our beloved country's name, reflecting our commitment to authentic Ethiopian travel experiences.

What started as a small family-run business has grown into one of Ethiopia's most respected travel agencies, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe in responsible tourism that benefits local communities, preserves cultural heritage, and protects Ethiopia's stunning natural landscapes.
